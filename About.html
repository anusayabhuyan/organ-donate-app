Information about Organ, Eye, and Tissue Donation

Every day, lives are saved and improved by the gift of life, sight and health. Even though 156 million people in the U.S. have registered as donors, we all need to sign up. people are waiting for an organ.

HOW DONATION WORKS

More than 155 million people have registered as organ donors, but only about 3 in 1,000 can actually become donors when they die. Learn about the process of becoming a deceased organ donor.
